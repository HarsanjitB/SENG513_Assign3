Welcome to Powered Snakes - Multiplayer

Instructions: Use W, A, S, D keys for Player 1 and Arrow keys for Player 2 to control the snakes. Try to eat as much food (green blocks) as possible without colliding with the walls, black blocks, or the other snake. Eating green food blocks will increase your point score. Your point score can be treated as lives should you hit a black block on the grid. If you have a score of zero and hit a black block, the game is over.
